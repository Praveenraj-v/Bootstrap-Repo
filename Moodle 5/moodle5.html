NISSAN SKYLINE GT-R34

The Nissan Skyline GT-R is a sports car based on the Nissan Skyline range. The first cars named "Skyline GT-R" were produced between 1969 and 1972 under the model code KPGC10, and were successful in Japanese touring car racing events. This model was followed by a brief production run of second-generation cars, under model code KPGC110, in 1973 and the GT-R name was revived in 1989 as the BNR32 ("R32") Skyline GT-R.
